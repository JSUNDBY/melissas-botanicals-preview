The transition

How we move you off WordPress, safely

Migration is the part of a rebuild that gets people nervous. Here's exactly how the transition works so nothing is lost, nothing breaks at launch, and you have time to actually look at the new system before you're committed to it.

1
Your current site keeps running
During the build, melissasbotanicals.com stays exactly where it is. Customers shop normally, orders flow normally. Nothing on the live site is touched.
2
We migrate your data to staging
All 115 products, customer accounts, and order history get exported from WooCommerce and imported into Medusa. The new system is built on a staging URL where only you and Dawn can see it.
3
You test it before launch
You spend as much time as you need on staging, adding test products, processing test orders, exploring the new admin. We don't go live until you've signed off on every flow.
4
Launch with old site as backup
Traffic moves to the new site. Your old WordPress install stays online for 90 days as backup, if anything unexpected comes up, full fallback exists. After 90 clean days, we archive it with a complete export.

Conversion, turn visitors into customers

Product Finder Quiz
High-conversion funnel
"Tell us what's going on. We'll show you what to use."
An interactive 5-question quiz that takes a visitor from "what oil do I need?" to a personalized formula recommendation. What are you trying to address? How does it show up? Excellent for paid ad funnels.

60-90 second videos showing how to use Pain Remedy, the Facial Rejuvenation system, the deodorants, the hydrosols. One per top-seller. Embedded on the product page and uploaded to YouTube. Products with video on the page convert 30-50% better than products without.
AI Aromatherapy Assistant
24/7 customer service
A chat assistant trained on your knowledge.
An always-available assistant on the site that answers customer questions using your usage guide, chemistry reference, and product catalog. Recommends products by symptom.
