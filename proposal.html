Pre-Proposal

Melissa's Botanicals

A new website · The admin you already know · Growth services
May 18, 2026

You built something rare.

Twenty-seven years of clinical aromatherapy practice. The first 100% certified organic essential oils in the American market. A catalog of 115 products sourced from cooperatives in France, Bulgaria, Australia, Corsica, and Tunisia. Recognition in France as Formulateur en aromathérapie scientifique et médicale. A boutique in Bloomington that customers describe as a French apothecary in the Midwest.

The website doesn't reflect any of that yet. It works — but it's holding the business back from doing what it could be doing. It's slow on phones. It doesn't show up properly in search. It can't be updated without a developer. And the customer landing on it has to scroll through 115 products to find the one thing they came for.

What we're proposing is a new website. Not a tune-up of the current one — a complete new build on modern infrastructure, ready to scale with the business. The only thing that stays the same is the WooCommerce admin you log into to manage products. Everything customers see is built from scratch.

This document outlines how the new site comes together, plus the optional growth services you can layer in alongside it. We've kept the core build separate from the growth services so you can choose the scope that fits this season.

Working model

Modular scope, one deposit, one delivery

The project is built as a foundation plus a menu of optional growth services. You select which growth services to include up front; pricing is settled once the scope is settled.

The new website is the core build — a complete customer-facing site that takes the catalog from "needs a developer for every change" to "you run it yourself." Every engagement starts here.

The growth services are optional. They cover the pieces that compound: video, content, email automation, structured data, reviews, and more. Most clients choose two or three to launch with.

Platform additions are larger, optional capabilities — wholesale portal, subscription products, the option to retire WordPress entirely later. These get scoped separately if you want them.

One deposit, one delivery date. Whatever you choose gets built, tested, and launched together as the new site — not in fragments.

Where your store lives

A new site on a faster home, the admin you know

Your new website lives on Cloudflare — the same global delivery network used by some of the largest sites on the internet — while your existing WooCommerce admin stays exactly where it is. You log in the way you do today; customers see a brand-new site that loads in under a second from anywhere in the world.

The simplest way to think about it: two halves of the same store. The half you log into stays familiar. The half your customers see is completely new — new code, new design, new infrastructure. Add a product in WooCommerce as you always have, and it appears on the new site automatically.

Optional · Pick what fits

Growth services menu

The pieces that turn a working website into one that compounds. Each is priced individually. Most clients choose two or three for the launch and add the rest later.

Brand Story Video
Trust + SEO
A short film about Melissa and the work.
A 3-5 minute hero video for the homepage and About page: your background, the French Medical Aromatherapy training, the sourcing relationships, what makes the formulas different. Narrated, scored, and edited. Why it matters: brand-story video on a homepage measurably increases time-on-site, trust, and conversion.
Product Education Videos
Conversion
Short demos for your top-seller products.
60-90 second videos showing how to use Pain Remedy, the Facial Rejuvenation system, the deodorants, the hydrosols. One per top-seller. Embedded on the product page and YouTube. Why it matters: products with video on the page convert 30-50% better than products without.
Editorial Content & Blog SEO
Organic traffic
Articles that bring people to the site from Google.
A series of in-depth articles targeting the searches your customers actually run: "essential oil for sleep," "natural acne treatment," "menopause hot flash relief." Each article is researched, written, and optimized to rank. Why it matters: this is the channel that compounds. One article published this year keeps bringing traffic for years.
Email Automation Flows
Revenue lift
Emails that recover sales and bring customers back.
Abandoned cart recovery, post-purchase follow-up, win-back for lapsed customers, welcome series for new subscribers. Set up in Klaviyo or your platform of choice. Why it matters: abandoned cart alone typically recovers 10-15% of would-be-lost revenue once it's running.
Reviews & UGC Integration
Conversion + SEO
Verified reviews on every product page.
Real customer reviews collected automatically after purchase, displayed on each product page, and included in the structured data Google reads. Why it matters: star ratings in search results dramatically increase clicks. Verified reviews on a page increase trust at the moment of purchase.
AI Aromatherapy Assistant
Customer service
A chat assistant trained on your usage guide.
An always-available assistant on the site that answers customer questions using your existing usage guide and chemistry reference chart. Recommends products by ailment. Why it matters: most customers leave without buying because they don't know which product to choose. This answers that question 24/7 without you doing it personally.
Press & Media Kit Page
Authority
One page for journalists, bloggers, and partners.
A polished resource page with your bio, high-resolution brand assets, product shot library, press history, and contact for media inquiries. Why it matters: it makes it easy for press to write about you accurately. The current site doesn't have one — every inquiry becomes a back-and-forth.
Condition-Based Landing Pages
Conversion + SEO
Pages for what people are actually searching for.
Dedicated pages built around the conditions your products address — "Natural Pain Relief," "Acne & Skin Conditions," "Menopause Support," "Sleep & Anxiety." Each page recommends the right products and ranks for that condition in search. Why it matters: people don't search for "essential oil," they search for what they want fixed.
Optional · Larger additions

Platform additions

Bigger pieces that get scoped separately. None of these are required for the rebuild — they're capabilities you can layer in when the timing is right.

Modern Admin Migration
Optional upgrade
Replace the WordPress admin with a cleaner, modern interface.
For when you're ready to leave WordPress entirely. Migrate your product catalog, customer accounts, and order history from WooCommerce to Medusa — a modern, open-source store engine with a cleaner admin and no plugin treadmill. The customer-facing Cloudflare site stays exactly the same; only the back-end you log into changes. Why consider it: a calmer admin experience, lower long-term maintenance, owned forever, no license fees.
Wholesale / B2B Portal
New revenue
A separate experience for retailers and practitioners.
Approved-account login, wholesale pricing, bulk order minimums, and a separate catalog view for retail partners. Distinct from the retail storefront so the two don't get confused. Why consider it: practitioner and retail wholesale is often higher-margin and more predictable than retail.
Subscription Products
Recurring revenue
Auto-ship for the formulas customers reorder.
Customer-managed subscriptions on top sellers: monthly, quarterly, or custom intervals. They can pause, skip, or change formulas from their account. Why consider it: subscription revenue is the most stable kind. It also signals reliability to investors and partners.
Practitioner Program
Authority + Revenue
Verified accounts for aromatherapists and clinicians.
A program for the aromatherapists, nurses, and naturopaths already using your formulas in practice. Verification, special pricing, clinical reference materials, and a directory listing on the site. Why consider it: it formalizes the credibility you already have in the practitioner community.
Affiliate & Referral Program
Growth channel
Let your existing fans earn for referring customers.
Trackable referral links, commission tiers, an affiliate dashboard, and automated payouts. Designed for the customers and practitioners who already recommend you. Why consider it: the highest-converting traffic is word-of-mouth. This rewards it and makes it trackable.
Photography & Visual Refresh
Brand
Standardized, on-brand product imagery.
A full refresh of product photography — consistent lighting, backgrounds, and presentation across the entire catalog. Includes lifestyle shots for hero use. Scoped per shoot day. Why consider it: the current photos are a mix of formats and ages. Unified imagery is what makes a catalog feel premium.
