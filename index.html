What this solves

This is not simply a website rebuild.

It is a modernization of how customers find your formulas, how they come to trust them, and how you run the business behind it. The presentation that follows lays out the work in four short documents.

For your customers

Discovery, not scrolling.

A site that helps a visitor find the right formula for their life in seconds, not a wall of 115 products to scroll past. Search, intelligent filters, and condition-based pathways replace the current single-page catalog.

For your operation

A calmer admin.

Adding a product becomes a two-minute task instead of a half-day project. The interface you log into is purpose-built for an ecommerce business — no Elementor, no plugins to update, no developer dependency for routine changes.

For the brand

A presentation worthy of the work.

Twenty-seven years of clinical aromatherapy, French training, and small-batch sourcing — all reflected in a site that feels as considered as the formulas themselves.

For long-term growth

Foundations that compound.

Modern commerce levers — programmatic SEO, email automation, founder-led content, subscriptions — designed to grow visibility and revenue across years, not weeks.
